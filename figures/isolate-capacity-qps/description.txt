QPS and LMCache Effects on Throughput, Per-Agent LLM Time, and Completion Tokens

This figure compares three explicit `mini-swe-agent` source runs: `trail` at `qps0_5`, `trail` at `qps0_6`, and `trail-lmcache` at `qps0_6`.
